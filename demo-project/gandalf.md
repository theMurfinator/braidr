## The Grey Pilgrim (2)
Gandalf the Grey — wanderer, counselor, and keeper of secrets. He alone understands the full scope of the threat.

1. ==**The Ring Identified**== #gandalf arrives at #bag_end after seventeen years of research. He confirms #frodo's ring is the #one_ring of #sauron. He instructs Frodo to leave the Shire and arranges to meet at the #prancing_pony in #bree.
   - Years searching archives in Minas Tirith
   - The scroll of Isildur — the Ring's description matches
   - Testing the Ring in fire
   - "I wish it need not have happened in my time." — "So do I. So do all who live to see such times."

2. Prisoner of Isengard — #gandalf rides to #isengard seeking counsel from #saruman. Discovers Saruman has turned to #sauron. Imprisoned on the pinnacle of #orthanc. Rescued by #gwaihir the eagle.
   - Saruman's offer: "Together we can rule"
   - The palantir's corruption
   - The moth — calling the Eagle
   - Gandalf arrives in Rivendell late, battered

## The Fellowship (3)
Guiding the nine walkers south — through darkness and fire.

3. The Council — At the #council_of_elrond, #gandalf reveals the full history of the #one_ring and the threat of #sauron. He counsels that the Ring must be destroyed, not hidden or wielded. He volunteers to guide the Fellowship.
   - "There is only one Lord of the Ring, and he does not share power."
   - Mapping the route south
   - The debate over the mountain pass vs. Moria

4. ==**The Bridge of Khazad-dum**== In #moria, the Fellowship awakens the #balrog of Morgoth. #gandalf confronts it on the Bridge of #khazad_dum. He shatters the bridge — but the Balrog's whip drags him into the abyss. "Fly, you fools!"
   - Reading the book of Mazarbul — Balin's doom
   - "This foe is beyond any of you."
   - The fall into darkness
   - The battle in the deep — falling and fighting for days

5. The Battle in the Deep — #gandalf and the #balrog fall for ten days through the roots of the mountain, into sunless water and up the Endless Stair to the peak of #zirakzigil. Gandalf slays the Balrog and dies. He is sent back.
   - Fighting in the water and in the tunnels
   - The Endless Stair — carved through the mountain
   - Three days on the peak — lightning and ice
   - "I threw down my enemy, and he fell from the high place"
   - Death, and a choice to return

## The White Rider (3)
Gandalf the White — returned with greater power and purpose. The chessmaster moves.

6. ==**Gandalf the White**== Reborn, #gandalf appears in #fangorn Forest to #aragorn, #legolas, and #gimli. He has been sent back with greater authority. He redirects them to #edoras to free King #theoden.
   - "I am Gandalf the White. And I come back to you now, at the turn of the tide."
   - Memory returning slowly — "Gandalf? Yes... that was what they used to call me."
   - Shadowfax answers his call

7. The Liberation of Theoden — At #edoras, #gandalf breaks #saruman's hold on King #theoden through #grima_wormtongue. The king wakes from years of enchantment. Gandalf warns of Saruman's army and rides for reinforcements.
   - Wormtongue expelled from the Golden Hall
   - Theoden: "I know your face... Gandalf?"
   - Gandalf rides for Erkenbrand and the Westfold men
   - "Look to my coming at first light on the fifth day. At dawn, look to the east."

8. ==**Helm's Deep — The Dawn**== #gandalf arrives at #helms_deep at sunrise with two thousand #rohirrim, breaking the #uruk_hai siege. The forest of #huorns finishes the rout. He turns his attention to #isengard and #saruman.
   - The white rider on the hill
   - The charge down the slope
   - Saruman's army trapped between cavalry and walking trees
   - Victory — but the war has only begun

## The War of the Ring (3)
The final gambit — orchestrating the end of the Third Age.

9. The Voice of Saruman — At #isengard (destroyed by #ents), #gandalf confronts #saruman in #orthanc. Saruman refuses to repent. #grima throws down the #palantir. Gandalf takes it — a tool and a risk.
   - The flooded ring of Isengard — Merry and Pippin feasting
   - Saruman's persuasive voice — nearly swaying Theoden
   - Gandalf breaks Saruman's staff
   - Pippin's secret look into the Palantir

10. The Siege of Gondor — #gandalf rides to #minas_tirith to organize the defense against #sauron's assault. #denethor, the steward, is falling into despair and madness through his #palantir. The siege begins — the army of #mordor at the gates.
    - Denethor: "Against the power that now arises there is no victory"
    - Gandalf takes command of the city's defense
    - The Witch-king breaks the gates of Minas Tirith
    - Denethor's pyre — Gandalf saves Faramir

11. ==**The Black Gate**== #gandalf and #aragorn lead seven thousand to the #black_gate of #mordor — not to win, but to draw #sauron's eye from #frodo. The Host of the West stands before Sauron's full army. Then the Ring is destroyed, and everything Sauron built falls to ruin.
    - The march to the Morannon
    - "For Frodo." — the charge
    - The Eagles arrive
    - Barad-dur collapses — the earth swallows Sauron's army
    - Gandalf on Gwaihir, rescuing Frodo and Sam from the fire
