## The Shire (3)
Frodo's ordinary world — a peaceful life in the Shire, unaware of the storm gathering in the East. Establishes what he stands to lose.

1. ==**A Long-Expected Party**== #bilbo's 111th birthday party in #hobbiton. Fireworks, speeches, and a vanishing act. Frodo inherits #bag_end and a mysterious gold #ring.
   - Bilbo's speech: "I don't know half of you half as well as I should like"
   - Gandalf's unease about the Ring
   - The neighborhood gossip about Bilbo's treasure

2. The Shadow of the Past — #gandalf returns to #bag_end after years of research. He reveals the #ring is the #one_ring of the Dark Lord #sauron. "Keep it secret. Keep it safe."
   - Gandalf tests the Ring in fire — the inscription appears
   - The story of Isildur's failure
   - Sam caught eavesdropping

3. Leaving the Shire — Frodo sells #bag_end and sets out with #sam, #merry, and #pippin through the countryside. A black shape on the road behind them. #nazgul.
   - The Fox's puzzlement at walking hobbits
   - Hiding under tree roots from the Black Rider
   - Farmer Maggot's mushrooms

## The Road to Rivendell (4)
The journey east — from comfort to peril. Frodo learns the world is far more dangerous than he imagined.

4. ==**The Prancing Pony**== Frodo meets #aragorn (Strider) at the inn in #bree. A mysterious ranger who knows too much. The #ring slips onto Frodo's finger by accident, revealing him to the #nazgul.
   - Butterbur's delayed letter from Gandalf
   - "All that is gold does not glitter"
   - Aragorn: "Are you frightened?" — "Yes." — "Not nearly frightened enough."

5. Weathertop — The #nazgul attack on #weathertop at night. The #witch_king stabs Frodo with a #morgul_blade. #aragorn drives them off with fire.
   - The ruins of the old watchtower
   - Five Riders closing in
   - Frodo puts on the Ring — sees the Wraith-world
   - The shard works its way toward his heart

6. Flight to the Ford — Racing against time as the #morgul_blade poison spreads. #arwen finds them and carries Frodo to safety. The flood at the #ford_of_bruinen destroys the pursuing #nazgul.
   - Frodo fading, turning translucent
   - Arwen's defiance: "If you want him, come and claim him"
   - The river rises against the Nine

7. ==**The Council of Elrond**== At #rivendell, #elrond convenes a council of all free peoples. The debate over what to do with the #one_ring — no one can wield it, it cannot be hidden, it must be destroyed in #mount_doom. "I will take it. I will take the Ring to Mordor."
   - Boromir's case for using the Ring against Sauron
   - Aragorn reveals himself as Isildur's heir
   - The breaking of Narsil and reforging as Anduril
   - Sam insists on coming: "Don't you leave him, Samwise Gamgee"

## The Fellowship (3)
Traveling south with the nine companions. The burden of the Ring grows heavier.

8. The Mines of Moria — The Fellowship enters #moria after the #watcher_in_the_water seals the doors. Three days in darkness. Frodo sees #gollum following them.
   - "Speak, friend, and enter"
   - The tomb of Balin
   - Frodo's mithril coat revealed
   - Gollum's eyes in the dark

9. ==**The Bridge of Khazad-dum**== The #balrog appears. #gandalf faces it on the bridge of #khazad_dum. "You shall not pass!" — and falls into shadow. The Fellowship stumbles out, shattered.
   - Drums in the deep
   - The cave troll fight
   - Gandalf's last stand
   - Frodo's grief — the first real loss

10. Lothlorien — Rest in the elven realm of #lothlorien. #galadriel shows Frodo her #mirror — visions of the Shire burning, the Eye of #sauron. She refuses the Ring: "I pass the test."
    - The light of Earendil — Galadriel's gift to Frodo
    - The boats of Lorien
    - Frodo begins to withdraw from the others

## The Breaking of the Fellowship (3)
The Ring's corruption reaches a crisis. Frodo must choose to go on alone.

11. ==**Boromir's Fall**== At #amon_hen, #boromir tries to take the #one_ring from Frodo by force. "It is mine by right!" Frodo puts on the Ring, sees the Eye, and flees.
    - Boromir's desperation — the Ring's corruption
    - Frodo on the Seat of Seeing — the Eye
    - The moment of decision

12. The Breaking — Frodo decides to go to #mordor alone. He takes a boat across the river. #sam sees him leaving and wades in after him, nearly drowning. "I made a promise, Mr. Frodo."
    - Frodo tries to leave without being noticed
    - Sam can't swim
    - "Come then, Mr. Frodo. Let's go on together."

13. The Dead Marshes — Frodo and #sam cross the #dead_marshes with #gollum as their reluctant guide. Faces of the dead beneath the water. The #nazgul overhead on fell beasts.
    - Gollum's two personalities — Smeagol vs Gollum
    - "Don't follow the lights"
    - The Ring growing heavier

## Mordor (4)
The final approach to Mount Doom. Everything stripped away except will.

14. The Black Gate — They reach the #black_gate of #mordor. Impassable. #gollum offers another way in — through #ithilien and the secret stair.
    - The vast army marching through the gate
    - Frodo's temptation to put on the Ring
    - Trusting Gollum — Sam's misgivings

15. ==**Shelob's Lair**== #gollum leads them into #shelob's lair. The giant spider attacks. Frodo uses the #light_of_earendil but is stung and paralyzed. Sam believes Frodo is dead and takes the #one_ring.
    - The stench and the darkness
    - Gollum's betrayal revealed
    - Sam fights Shelob alone with Sting
    - Taking the Ring: "I'll carry it for a while"

16. The Tower of Cirith Ungol — Sam rescues Frodo from the #orcs in the tower. Returns the #one_ring. They disguise themselves as orcs and march into #mordor.
    - Sam hears Frodo is alive
    - Fighting through the orc garrison
    - Frodo: "Give me the Ring" — a flash of Sauron in his eyes
    - The plains of Gorgoroth

17. ==**Mount Doom**== The climb up #mount_doom. Frodo can barely walk. At the #crack_of_doom, Frodo claims the #one_ring: "The Ring is mine." #gollum bites off his finger and falls into the fire. The Ring is destroyed. #sauron falls.
    - Sam carries Frodo up the mountain
    - "I can't carry it for you, but I can carry you"
    - The final failure of will — and the eucatastrophe
    - The Eagles rescue them from the eruption
