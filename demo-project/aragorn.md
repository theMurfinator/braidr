## The Ranger (3)
Aragorn in exile — hiding his true identity, serving the free peoples in secret. The reluctant king.

1. ==**The Ranger of the North**== Aragorn patrols the wilderness near Bree, watching for servants of the Enemy. Years of solitude, a broken sword, and a love left behind in Rivendell. He receives word from Gandalf: a hobbit is coming with something important. #aragorn #bree #rivendell #gandalf
   - The weight of his lineage — Isildur's heir, Isildur's shame
   - The Rangers: unseen protectors of the Shire

2. Strider Revealed — At the Prancing Pony in Bree, Aragorn reveals himself to Frodo. Convinces the hobbits to trust him. They set out for Rivendell with the Nazgul close behind. #prancing_pony #bree #frodo #rivendell #nazgul
   - "You can no longer wait for the wizard, Frodo. They are coming."
   - The letter from Gandalf confirming his identity

3. Weathertop — Aragorn defends the hobbits against five Nazgul on Weathertop. Drives them off with fire and sword, but not before Frodo is stabbed by the Witch-king. The race to Rivendell begins. #aragorn #nazgul #weathertop #frodo #witch_king
   - Athelas — kingsfoil — the healing herb
   - Carrying Frodo through the wilderness

## The Fellowship's Journey (3)
From Rivendell south — Aragorn must lead after Gandalf falls.

4. ==**The Council and the Reforging**== At the Council of Elrond, Aragorn presents the shards of Narsil. The sword is reforged as Anduril, Flame of the West. He commits to the quest and the Fellowship of Nine is formed. #council_of_elrond #aragorn #narsil #anduril
   - "If by my life or death I can protect you, I will."
   - Elrond's challenge: earn the throne or lose Arwen forever
   - Boromir: "Gondor has no king. Gondor needs no king."

5. Through Moria and Lorien — The Fellowship journeys south. In Moria, Gandalf falls fighting the Balrog. Aragorn must take command. In Lothlorien, Galadriel tests his resolve. #moria #gandalf #balrog #lothlorien #galadriel
   - Stepping into leadership reluctantly
   - "Let the Ring-bearer decide" — Aragorn refuses to choose for Frodo

6. ==**The Breaking at Amon Hen**== Boromir falls to the Ring's temptation and attacks Frodo. The Uruk-hai assault the Fellowship at Amon Hen. Boromir redeems himself defending Merry and Pippin, but is mortally wounded. "I would have followed you, my brother. My captain. My king." #boromir #frodo #uruk_hai #amon_hen #merry #pippin
   - Boromir's confession and death — the three arrows
   - Aragorn's choice: follow Frodo, or save Merry and Pippin?

## The Two Towers (4)
The hunt across Rohan and the battle for Helm's Deep. Aragorn begins to become king.

7. The Hunt — Aragorn, Legolas, and Gimli pursue the Uruk-hai across Rohan for three days without rest. They meet Eomer and the Riders, who killed the orcs — but the hobbits escaped into Fangorn. #aragorn #legolas #gimli #uruk_hai #rohan #eomer #fangorn
   - Forty-five leagues in four days
   - "The hobbits are alive" — tracks leading into the forest

8. ==**The Return of Gandalf**== In Fangorn Forest, the three hunters encounter Gandalf — returned as Gandalf the White. He sends them to Edoras to free Theoden from Saruman's control. #fangorn #aragorn #legolas #gimli #gandalf #edoras #theoden #saruman
   - "I am Gandalf the White. And I come back to you now, at the turn of the tide."
   - New hope — the war can be won

9. Edoras and the Golden Hall — At Edoras, Aragorn helps free King Theoden from Wormtongue's influence. Theoden rallies Rohan for war. The people flee to Helm's Deep. #edoras #aragorn #theoden #grima_wormtongue #helms_deep
   - Theoden wakes: "Dark have been my dreams of late"
   - Eowyn's eyes following Aragorn

10. ==**Helm's Deep**== The Battle of Helm's Deep. Ten thousand Uruk-hai against three hundred men. The wall is breached. All seems lost — then Gandalf arrives at dawn with the Rohirrim. Victory. #battle_of_helms_deep #uruk_hai #gandalf #rohirrim
    - The deeping wall explosion
    - "Ride out with me" — the dawn charge
    - Gandalf and Eomer crest the hill

## The Return of the King (4)
From doubt to destiny. Aragorn claims his birthright.

11. The Paths of the Dead — Aragorn takes the Paths of the Dead beneath the White Mountains, summoning the Oathbreakers — ghosts of men who betrayed Isildur. Only the heir of Isildur can command them. #aragorn #paths_of_the_dead #oathbreakers #isildur
    - Gimli's terror in the dark
    - Aragorn begins to command like a king

12. ==**The Battle of the Pelennor Fields**== Aragorn arrives at Minas Tirith by ship, unfurling the banner of the King. The Army of the Dead sweeps the battlefield. Theoden falls. Eowyn slays the Witch-king. The tide turns. #aragorn #minas_tirith #army_of_the_dead #battle_of_pelennor_fields
    - The black ships on the river — despair turns to hope
    - "The hands of the king are the hands of a healer"
    - Healing Eowyn, Faramir, and Merry in the Houses of Healing

13. The Last Debate — At Minas Tirith, Aragorn and Gandalf plan a desperate gamble: march on the Black Gate with a small army to distract Sauron from Frodo's mission. A suicide feint. #minas_tirith #aragorn #gandalf #black_gate #sauron #frodo
    - "Let us draw Sauron's Eye to us"
    - Aragorn uses the Palantir — challenges Sauron directly

14. ==**The Coronation**== After Sauron's fall, Aragorn is crowned King Elessar at Minas Tirith. Arwen arrives. "This day does not belong to one man, but to all." The new age begins. #sauron #aragorn #minas_tirith #arwen
    - Gandalf places the crown
    - Aragorn bows to the hobbits: "My friends. You bow to no one."
    - The reunion with Arwen — the choice fulfilled
